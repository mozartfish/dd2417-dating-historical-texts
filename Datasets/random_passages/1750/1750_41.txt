I have bespoken, sir, the civilities of your sisters, of your family: you forbid them not? You expect not an answer, madam, to that question. My sisters will be glad, and so will their lords, to attend you wherever you please, with a hope to make England agreeable to you. How long do you propose to stay in Italy, sir? It is not possible for me to determine. Are you not apprehensive of danger to your person? I am not. You ought to be. No danger shall deter me from doing what I think to be right. If my motives justify me, I cannot fear. Do you wish me, sir, to stay in England till your return? A question so home put, disturbed him. Was it a prudent one in the lady?
